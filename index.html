The Best Holidays Start Here!

Located in Hyderabad, Hotel Real Cottage has a garden. Among the facilities at this property are room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a microwave, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a terrace and a private bathroom with a shower.
